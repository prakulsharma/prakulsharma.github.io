Smart Street Light

A smart and efficient street light

Overview

This project is developed to demonstrate an efficient way of using street lights that results in lesser energy consumption and lower running costs. An 8051 microcontroller is an 8-bit microcontroller. It is built with 40 pins DIP (dual inline package), 4kb of ROM storage and 128 bytes of RAM storage with 2 16-bit timers. These street lights are designed to turn off when a particular level of sunlight is detected by the light sensor or LDR. The simplicity of the circuit creates opportunity for this setup to have various other applications as well, for instance, in security and surveillance.

Technology used

Embedded C, AT89C52 microcontroller
